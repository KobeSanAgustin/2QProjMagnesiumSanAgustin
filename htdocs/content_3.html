Normal Bullying vs Cyberbullying

Let us not only learn about the effects of cyberbullying but also how it is different from regular bullying and how there are also similarities between them. On the left there will be the effects of normal bullying and on the right will be the effects of cyberbullying.

Bullying

  • Affected academic performace
  • Psychological problems (Depression, Anxiety, Low Self-esteem etc.)
  • Behavioral changes (Appetite loss, lack of interest in activities they used to enjoy etc.)
  • Physical injury that comes from violence in the form of kicking, punching, hitting, shoving and more
  • Damaged relationships and reputation

Cyberbullying

  • Affected academic performace
  • Psychological problems (Depression, Anxiety, Low Self-esteem etc.)
  • Behavioral changes (Appetite loss, lack of interest in activities they used to enjoy etc.)
  • Physical injury in the form of insomnia, gastrointestinal issues or harmful eating patterns
  • Damaged relationships and reputation
