Tips to Avoid and Prevent Cyberbullying

There are many different ways to avoid and prevent cyberbullying that is why this will be split into 3 different categories, on the right of the category you want to explore and it will give you additional information for that specific category.

Ignoring
Talking
Ending

If the cyberbullying isn't serious and is just some trolls looking to mess with people you can solve this issue by blocking them on that site and ignoring them.

Once the cyberbullying gets more severe and affects your mental health you can talk with someone you trust about your problems to help get it off your chest.

To finally end the cyberbullying as much as you can, you can report this to the local authorities or people in power if this is violating you as a person. You can also make a new account or use another social media to avoid these bullies.
