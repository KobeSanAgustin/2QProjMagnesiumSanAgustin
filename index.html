Cyberbullying's Presence in the Present

Introduction to Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying was already a problem before the pandemic started, but now where almost everything is in digital form, it has become much more frequent, not only in online schooling but also media websites like “Facebook”, “Twitter”, or others. Though the act of cyberbullying may not be as physical as normal bullying, its effects can be devastating to the people affected.
